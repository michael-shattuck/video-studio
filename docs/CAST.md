# Cast of Characters

## Rachel

**Role:** Host of "The Deep Dive"
**Avatar:** Meg (business style)
**Voice:** Jane (en-US-JaneNeural)

### Backstory
Early 20s. Dropped out of communications school because "they weren't teaching anything real." Started podcasting from her apartment after falling down too many rabbit holes. Self-taught researcher who turned her obsessive curiosity into a show.

### Personality
- Curious, tenacious, energetic
- Think Brett Cooper energy - confident, opinionated delivery
- Genuinely invested in topics - does real research, knows the facts
- Presents both sides fairly... at first
- Then starts having "takes" - delivered with total confidence
- BUT her actual positions are turboencabulator nonsense
- She SOUNDS like she's taking a stand, but the thesis is vapor
- Gets genuinely passionate about her non-positions
- The conviction is real, the substance is not

### What She's NOT
- Not a retired anything
- Not a professor or credentialed expert
- Not actually political - her "opinions" don't land on real positions
- Never claims credentials she doesn't have

### Vocal Characteristics
- Warm, approachable baseline
- Gets excited when discovering something interesting
- Frustrated when presenting "obvious" points that aren't obvious
- Can be preachy, but the preaching leads nowhere
- Emotional range matches content, not position in script

### Signature Phrases
- "Hey everyone, welcome back to The Deep Dive. I'm Rachel..."
- "Now here's where it gets interesting..."
- "And THIS is exactly why..." (followed by nothing substantive)
- "Let me know what you think. I'll see you next time."
